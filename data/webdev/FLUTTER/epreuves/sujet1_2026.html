flowchart TD
    A[Application Flutter] --> B[Widgets (UI)]
    B --> C[Rendering Engine (Skia)]
    C --> D[Platform Channel]
    D --> E[Android (Kotlin/Java)]
    D --> F[iOS (Swift/Objective-C)]
    D --> G[Web / Desktop]
