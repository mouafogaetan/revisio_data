Exercise 8: Composition (25 marks)

Choose ONE of the following topics and write a short essay of about 150-200 words.

Topic A: Social Issues

"Juvenile delinquency is becoming a serious problem in our society."

👉 Discuss the causes of this problem and suggest two solutions to help young people stay out of trouble.

Topic B: Urban Life

"More and more people are moving from villages to cities."

👉 Explain the advantages and disadvantages of living in a big city versus a rural area.

Topic C: Civic Responsibility

"Respecting the elderly is a fundamental value in our culture."

👉 Write a short story or a persuasive text explaining why we should respect our ancestors and the elderly.

Evaluation criteria: Relevance to topic (5 pts), Grammar & Spelling (10 pts), Vocabulary richness (5 pts), Coherence & Organisation (5 pts).
