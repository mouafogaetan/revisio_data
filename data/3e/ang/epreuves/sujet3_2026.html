SECTION C : WRITING (40 marks)

Composition

40 marks

Choose ONE of the following topics. Write a short essay of 150–200 words.

Topic A : Civic Engagement

“Some young people in your community do not participate in the election process or in community development. As a youth leader, write a speech to encourage them to vote and respect the national symbols.”

Include vocabulary: ballot, duty, flag, anthem, tolerance, democracy.

Topic B : Community Life

“Your school is organizing a ‘Unity Week’ to fight against tribalism and promote living together. Write an article for the school magazine explaining why respecting elders and embracing bilingualism are important for peace in Cameroon.”

Include at least 2 conjunctions (although, because, so that) and one relative clause.

Evaluation criteria: Content relevance (15), grammar & connectors (10), vocabulary richness (10), coherence & organisation (5).
