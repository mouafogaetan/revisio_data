📖 PART ONE: GRAMMAR (15 marks)
1️⃣ Choose the correct option:
"You look exhausted. You ______ see a doctor if the symptoms persist."
  • A. could
  • B. should
  • C. may
  • D. might
✔ Correct answer: B. should
🔹 "Should" is used to give strong advice or recommendation. "Could/may" express possibility, not advice.
2️⃣ Complete with the correct form:
"If people _______ (to follow) hygiene measures, the spread of diseases _______ (to reduce) significantly."
  • A. follow / will reduce
  • B. followed / would reduce
  • C. follows / reduces
  • D. will follow / reduce
✔ Correct answer: A. follow / will reduce
🔹 First conditional (real possibility): If + present simple, will + infinitive.
3️⃣ Which sentence expresses a strong obligation (rule)?
  • A. You should take your prescription twice a day.
  • B. You may stay at home if you are sick.
  • C. Patients must wear a mask in the waiting room.
  • D. You can try some herbal tea for your cough.
✔ Correct answer: C. Patients must wear a mask in the waiting room.
🔹 "Must" indicates an obligation or rule. "Should" = advice, "may/can" = permission/possibility.
4️⃣ Reorder the words to make a polite question:
"me / could / you / tell / the symptoms / of malaria / please ?"
  • A. Could you tell me the symptoms of malaria please?
  • B. Tell me the symptoms could you please?
  • C. The symptoms of malaria could you tell me please?
  • D. You could tell me the symptoms of malaria please?
✔ Correct answer: A
🔹 Polite request using modal "could" + subject + verb.
📖 PART TWO: VOCABULARY (15 marks)
5️⃣ Match each word with its definition:
  • 1. Vaccine     A. A written order from a doctor for medicine
  • 2. Prescription   B. A substance that stimulates the immune system
  • 3. Hygiene       C. Practices to maintain health and prevent disease
✔ Answers: 1→B, 2→A, 3→C
🔹 Vaccine = prevention (immunity). Prescription = doctor’s note for medication. Hygiene = cleanliness for health.
6️⃣ Complete with: symptoms / treatment / protection / auto-medication
"Taking drugs without a doctor’s advice is called _______. Common _______ of the flu include fever and headache. The best _______ is to wash hands regularly."
✔ auto-medication / symptoms / protection
🔹 Auto-medication = self-treatment without prescription.
7️⃣ Which word is NOT related to prevention campaigns?
  • A. vaccine
  • B. hygiene
  • C. unemployment
  • D. protection
✔ C. unemployment
🔹 Unemployment is an economic/social issue, not directly linked to health prevention.
📖 PART THREE: READING COMPREHENSION (15 marks)
📄 TEXT: Fighting Malaria in Rural Areas

Malaria remains a major public health challenge in many tropical countries. Prevention campaigns have been organized to distribute insecticide-treated bed nets and to educate communities on eliminating stagnant water where mosquitoes breed. Health officials strongly recommend that families sleep under bed nets every night. If a person develops a high fever, headache, or vomiting, they should get tested immediately. Early diagnosis and treatment save lives. However, some people still rely on traditional remedies or ignore early symptoms, which leads to severe complications. The government has launched a "Zero Malaria Starts with Me" campaign, encouraging every citizen to take responsibility for their environment and to visit health centers as soon as symptoms appear.
8️⃣ According to the text, what are two recommended ways to prevent malaria?
✔ Using insecticide-treated bed nets & eliminating stagnant water.
🔹 (Line 3–4: "distribute insecticide-treated bed nets" / "eliminating stagnant water")
9️⃣ What happens when people ignore early symptoms or rely only on traditional remedies?
✔ It leads to severe complications.
🔹 Last sentence of the passage: "... leads to severe complications."
🔟 Find a synonym in the text for "quick identification of an illness".

📖 PART FOUR: COMPOSITION (15 marks)
✍️ Choose ONE topic and write a short paragraph (80–100 words).
  • Topic A: Your school is organizing a health awareness day. Write a speech to convince students to adopt good hygiene habits (washing hands, covering mouth when coughing, etc.).
  • Topic B: Imagine you are a doctor. Give three pieces of advice to a patient who has flu symptoms. Use modals (should, must, have to).
✔ SAMPLE (Topic A):
Dear fellow students, good hygiene is our first shield against diseases. You should wash your hands regularly with soap, especially before eating. Also, remember to cover your mouth and nose with your elbow when you cough or sneeze. If you feel sick, you must wear a mask to protect others. Let us build a healthier school environment together! These small actions save lives.
