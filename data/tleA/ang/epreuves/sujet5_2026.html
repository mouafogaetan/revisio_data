Media, Technology & Communication

Terminale A – Durée : 1h30
📖 Theme : Digital World & Customer Service 🎯 Grammar : Past Perfect / Reported Speech
📖 Part 1: Reading Comprehension (08 pts)
📄 Document
"When Sarah ordered a smartphone online last month, she hadn't checked the seller's reputation. Two weeks later, she received a broken device. The customer service told her: 'We will refund you if you return the product.' However, after returning it, she never got her money back. Her account had been hacked the week before, which complicated the situation."
❓ Question 1
Why didn't Sarah get a refund immediately?
  • A. She forgot to ask for it.
  • B. She hadn't checked the seller's reputation AND her account had been hacked.
  • C. The phone was not broken.
  • D. She didn't want to return the product.

    • ✅ Réponse : B. She hadn't checked the seller's reputation AND her account had been hacked.

    🔍 Justification : Le texte mentionne qu'elle n'avait pas vérifié la réputation du vendeur et que son compte avait été piraté.

    📖 Vocabulary in Context
    In the text, the word "hacked" means :
  • A. Protected illegally
  • B. Accessed without authorization
  • C. Repaired carefully
  • D. Bought with a discount

    • ✅ Réponse : B. Accessed without authorization

    🔍 "Hacked" signifie piraté, c'est-à-dire accéder à un système sans autorisation.

    🎯 Grammar in Action
    The customer service said: "We will refund you if you return the product."
    Transform this sentence into Reported Speech :
  • A. The customer service said that we will refund you if you return the product.
  • B. The customer service said that they would refund me if I returned the product.
  • C. The customer service said that they will refund me if I return the product.
  • D. The customer service said that we would refund you if you returned the product.

    • ✅ Réponse : B. The customer service said that they would refund me if I returned the product.

    🔍 Règle : Au discours indirect, 'will' devient 'would', et les pronoms changent selon le contexte.

    ✍️ Part 2: Grammar Mastery (06 pts)
    ⏳ Past Perfect (pluperfect)
    Complete the sentence with the correct form (Past Perfect simple):
    "Before he bought the new laptop, he _______________ (never / use) a MacBook."

    ✅ Réponse : had never used

    📘 Explication : Le Past Perfect (had + participe passé) exprime une action antérieure à une autre action passée.

    🔧 Error Correction
    Find and correct the error:
    "She didn't know that the store closed before she arrived."

    ✅ Correction : "She didn't know that the store had closed before she arrived."

    📘 La fermeture du magasin est antérieure à son arrivée, d'où l'usage du Past Perfect.

    📝 Part 3: Writing Task
    ✉️ Formal Email of Complaint
    🖥️ Situation: You bought a smartphone online. The battery life is very poor (less than 2 hours) and the screen has a scratch. The warranty is still valid.

    📌 Task: Write a formal email to the customer service. You must include:
    • The problem with the product (battery + screen).
    • A reference to the warranty.
    • A request for a refund or a replacement.

    ✅ Exemple de lettre :

    Subject: Complaint regarding faulty smartphone – Order #XT2024

    Dear Customer Service,

    I am writing to complain about a smartphone I purchased from your website on the 10th of March. The order number is #XT2024.
    Unfortunately, the product is defective. Firstly, the battery life lasts less than two hours even after a full charge. Secondly, I noticed a visible scratch on the screen when I unboxed it.
    As the product is still under warranty, I would like to request a full refund or a replacement with a new device.
    I look forward to your prompt reply.

    Yours faithfully,
    [Your Name]
