Sequence 1: Civic Responsibilities & Social Issues
📌 Durée : 1 heure ⭐ Coefficient : 2 🏫 Année scolaire : 2025-2026
📖 PART 1: READING COMPREHENSION (8 points)
Read the text below about a social issue and answer the following questions.

"In many communities, harmful traditional practices continue to affect young girls. Female genital mutilation (FGM) is one of these practices. The perpetrators are often family members who believe it is necessary for cultural or religious reasons. However, these acts are a severe abuse of rights. Organizations like UNICEF are working to educate communities about the dangers, which include severe bleeding and psychological trauma. Activists argue that those who practice FGM should be prosecuted by the law."
1.1 According to the text, who are the perpetrators of FGM?
1.2 Find in the text a synonym for "dangerous effects".
1.3 What solution does the text suggest to stop this practice?

✅ Corrigé

1.1 The perpetrators are often family members.
1.2 "Dangers" (severe bleeding and psychological trauma).
1.3 Prosecuting the perpetrators by law and educating communities.

📖 PART 2: VOCABULARY (6 points)
Match each word with its correct definition.
  • 1. Perpetrator    A. A formal request for something (like a job or a document)
  • 2. Application form    B. The subjects taught in a school
  • 3. Curriculum    C. A person who commits an illegal or harmful act

    • ✅ Réponses
    1 → C (Perpetrator: a person who commits an illegal act)
    2 → A (Application form: a formal request)
    3 → B (Curriculum: school subjects)

    📖 PART 3: GRAMMAR (6 points)
    Complete the sentences with the correct relative pronoun (who, which, that).
  • a. A citizen is someone ______ respects the laws of the country.
  • b. Female mutilation is a practice ______ should be banned everywhere.
  • c. The passport application, ______ I submitted yesterday, was approved.

    • ✅ Corrigé
    a. who (refers to a person)
    b. which/that (refers to a thing/practice)
    c. which (non-defining relative clause)

    ✍️ PART 4: WRITING (10 points)
    Choose one of the following topics:

    Topic 1: You are the Senior Prefect of your school. Write a speech to address the issue of school violence. In your speech, give two causes, two consequences, and two solutions. (Use linking words: because, therefore, however).

    Topic 2: You want to obtain your passport for the first time. Write a dialogue between you and a civil servant asking for information politely. (Use modals: can, could, may).

    ✅ Exemple (Topic 1)
    "Dear fellow students, today I want to talk about violence in our school. Because many students feel frustrated, they sometimes react with anger. Therefore, we see fights increasing. However, we can solve this by creating a mediation club. If we organize awareness campaigns, we will reduce conflicts. Let us choose peace."
