Part 3 : Written Expression (16 points)

Chapitres 7 & 8 : Production écrite argumentative

7

Choose one of the following subjects. Write a short essay of about 150 to 200 words. (16 pts: 10 for quality/accuracy + 6 for vocabulary/grammar)

Subject 1 :

"Juvenile delinquency is becoming a major concern in our cities." As a young citizen, what are the causes of this problem, and what solutions would you propose? (Use modal verbs: should/must, and gerunds/infinitives)

Subject 2 :

Describe your ideal place to live. Would you prefer a modern apartment in town or a traditional house in the village? Justify your choice using specific vocabulary (rent, landlord, slum, environment...).
