I

Reading Comprehension (8 points)

Read the text carefully and answer the following questions.

🗑️ The Burden of Waste in Urban Cameroon

In many Cameroonian cities, such as Douala and Yaoundé, waste management has become a critical environmental issue. Every day, tons of plastic bottles, household rubbish, and electronic waste are dumped in unauthorized landfills, contaminating the soil and water sources. Although the government has passed laws against littering, the lack of adequate infrastructure and public awareness often leads to non-compliance. "We cannot continue to ignore the problem," said Dr. Amina Diallo, an environmental scientist. "If we do not act now, the health consequences will be irreversible."

However, grassroots initiatives are bringing hope. In the city of Bafoussam, a local youth group called “Green Future” has launched a recycling project that transforms discarded plastic into paving tiles. The project has not only reduced waste but also created jobs for dozens of young people. “Recycling is not just about protecting nature,” explains Marc Kamga, the founder. “It is about changing our mentality. When we see waste as a resource, everything changes.” The group is now working with municipal authorities to set up sorting bins in public markets, demonstrating that civic responsibility can drive sustainable change.

1. According to the text, what are the two main causes of poor waste management in Cameroonian cities ? (2 pts)

2. Explain the expression : “see waste as a resource” in your own words. (2 pts)

3. What are the environmental and social benefits of the “Green Future” project in Bafoussam ? (Mention two benefits). (2 pts)

4. Do you think individual actions (like sorting garbage at home) can really solve the waste problem? Justify your answer in one sentence. (2 pts)

II

Language in Use (7 points)

🔧 Grammar 1 : Causative Form (have something done)

Complete the sentences using the correct form of have + object + past participle.

a. In our neighbourhood, we finally ____________________ (our garbage / collect) twice a week by the council.
b. Last month, the mayor ____________________ (the old recycling bins / replace) with smart ones.
c. If we want cleaner streets, we should ____________________ (toxic waste / remove) by specialists.

♻️ Grammar 2 : Gerund as Subject & Vocabulary

Rewrite the sentences starting with a gerund, then match the bold word with its definition.

“It is important to recycle plastic waste because it reduces pollution.”

➜ Recycling plastic waste reduces pollution.

a. It is beneficial to reduce the amount of single-use packaging.

Match the word "reduce" with : ☐ to use again ☐ to make smaller in quantity ☐ to convert waste into material

b. It is crucial to reuse glass jars instead of throwing them away.

⚖️ Grammar 3 : Conditional Type 1 (Real Possibility)

Complete the sentences using the correct verb forms.

If citizens _______ (to sort) their waste properly, recycling _______ (to become) more efficient.
Unless we _______ (to raise) awareness, the landfills _______ (to continue) to contaminate groundwater.
III

Writing Production (5 points)

Sujet :

As part of the “Eco-Schools” program, your headmaster has asked you, as Senior Prefect, to write a short speech (120–150 words) to be delivered during the morning assembly. In your speech, you will :

  • Explain one major environmental problem in your school (e.g., littering, plastic waste).
  • Suggest two practical solutions involving students.
  • Use at least one conditional sentence (type 1) and two vocabulary words from the lesson (recycle, reduce, waste, environment, civic duty).
