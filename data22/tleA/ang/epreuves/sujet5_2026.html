🔍 Partie 2 : Reading Comprehension (30 pts)

Document : User manual extract

How to file a complaint for a faulty online purchase

When you receive a defective product ordered online, follow these steps. First, check the seller's return and warranty policy. Most platforms require a complaint within 30 days of delivery. Second, take clear photos or videos of the defect. This serves as evidence. Third, write a formal email or use the customer service portal. Mention your order number, the issue, and attach the proof. If the seller refuses a refund or replacement, you can escalate the matter to a consumer protection agency. Remember to keep a copy of all communications. Many companies also have a live chat option, which is faster than email. However, always save the chat transcript.

Source: adapted from "Consumer Rights in the Digital Era" – 2024

Write a polite but firm email to the customer service department. Include: order reference (FX-237B), the problem, what you expect (refund or replacement), and attach evidence.
